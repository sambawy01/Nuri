## 1. Background: Education 2.0 Reform (نظام التعليم الجديد)

### Overview

Egypt's Education 2.0 (EDU 2.0) reform was initiated by the Ministry of Education in **2018-2019** under Minister Tarek Shawki, designed to modernize curriculum, pedagogy, and assessment in the digital age.

### Key Changes

- **Grades 1-3 (KG-3):** Originally used a multidisciplinary approach with books like "اكتشف" (Discover), "تواصل" (Connect), and integrated thematic axes (محاور). As of **2025-2026**, the Ministry has shifted back to a **unit-and-lesson structure** (وحدات ودروس) replacing the axis-and-topic system (محاور وموضوعات).
- **Grades 4-6:** The traditional subject-based system was maintained but progressively updated. Grade 4 was reformed in 2021-2022, Grade 5 in 2022-2023, and Grade 6 in 2023-2024. Further revisions came in 2025-2026 with a unified Arabic language book integrating all four language skills.
- **Curriculum Redesign 2025-2026:** The Minister of Education announced comprehensive changes to Arabic language, religious education, social studies, and mathematics curricula from Grade 4 through Grade 8 (الصف الثاني الإعدادي), effective from the 2025-2026 academic year. The Arabic curriculum was redesigned as a single integrated book emphasizing listening, reading, writing, and speaking.
- **Assessment:** Grades 1-3 are assessed in Arabic, Mathematics, and English only (these count toward the total grade). Grades 4-6 add Science and Social Studies to the graded subjects.

### Curriculum Framework Documents

The Egyptian Ministry of Education, through the **Curriculum & Instructional Materials Development Center (CCIMD)**, produced curriculum frameworks aligned with the "General Framework for the General and Technical Education Curricula 2018/2030." These include:
- Grade-level frameworks for Grades 1-3 (available in English and Arabic)
- Subject-specific frameworks for Grades 4-6 including:
  - Arabic Language Framework
  - Religious Education Framework (Islam and Christianity)
  - Values and Respect for Others Framework

All frameworks are available through the Education 2.0 Research & Documentation Project at edu2-egypt.com.

### Official Textbook Source

The Ministry publishes all official textbooks through the **Electronic Library** (المكتبة الإلكترونية) at ellibrary.moe.gov.eg/books/.

## 2. Arabic Language (اللغة العربية) — Grades 1-6

### General Structure (2025-2026 Revised Curriculum)

All grades now follow a **3-4 unit structure per semester** (الترم). Each unit integrates:
- **Listening lesson** (درس استماع)
- **Reading lessons** (دروس قراءة) — stories, informational texts
- **Grammar** (النحو / القواعد النحوية) — introduced from Grade 3
- **Spelling/Dictation** (الإملاء)
- **Speaking activities** (التحدث)
- **Writing** (الكتابة / التعبير)
- **Poetry texts** (نصوص شعرية)
- **Family reading** (قراءة أسرية) — take-home shared reading activity

The 2025-2026 reform increased poetry texts to **3 per semester** (up from 2) and reduced written expression topics to **2 per semester**.

### Grade 1 — الصف الأول الابتدائي

**Curriculum Version:** New system (نظام جديد), revised 2025-2026
**Structure:** 4 units in Term 1 (الترم الأول)

#### Focus
- **Letter recognition and phonics** — learning all 28 Arabic letters
- **Listening comprehension** through stories
- **Songs** (أناشيد) for engagement
- **Beginning reading** through simple connected texts

#### Term 1 Content

| Unit | Theme (Arabic) | Key Content |
|------|----------------|-------------|
| **Unit 1** | أسرتي (My Family) | Listening: "أميرة وأسرتها السعيدة" (Amira and Her Happy Family); Song: "أسرتي"; Letters: أ، ب، م، هـ، ج، د، خ، ط; Reader: "أحب أسرتي" (I Love My Family) |
| **Unit 2** | حيواناتي (My Animals) | Listening: "عالم الحيوان" (The Animal World); Song: "لوحتي الجديدة"; Letters: ل، س، ن، ر، ف، ك، ق; Reader: "ماذا يأكلون؟" (What Do They Eat?); Family reading: "التعاون سر النجاح" |
| **Unit 3** | جسمي (My Body) | Listening: "صندوق جدتي" (Grandma's Box); Song: "أحب جسمي"; Letters: ي، ع، ش، و، ح، ذ، ظ; Reader: "هذا جسمي" (This Is My Body) |
| **Unit 4** | _(continuing remaining letters and review)_ | Remaining letters and consolidation activities |

#### Skills Focus
- Phonemic awareness and letter-sound correspondence
- Oral vocabulary building through songs and stories
- Basic handwriting (نسخ/خط)
- Simple dictation of learned letters and words
- No formal grammar instruction at this level

- Negation words (أدوات النفي): لم، لن
- Prepositions (حروف الجر): عن، على
- Conjunctions (حروف العطف): ثم، فـ
- Distinguishing ت / ة / ه

| إملاء (Spelling) | Incorporating preposition letters (ف، ب، ك، ل) with the definite article (ال) |

| نحو | **الضمائر** (Pronouns); **أركان الجملة الاسمية: المبتدأ والخبر** (Nominal sentence components: subject and predicate) |

- **أنواع الكلمة** (Word types): اسم (noun), فعل (verb), حرف (particle)
- **علامات الاسم** (Noun markers)
- **المفرد والمثنى والجمع** (Singular, dual, plural)
- **الضمائر** (Pronouns)
- **الجملة الاسمية: المبتدأ والخبر** (Nominal sentence: subject & predicate) — **FIRST FORMAL INTRODUCTION**
- **المبتدأ والخبر المثنى وجمع المذكر السالم** (Subject/predicate with dual and sound masculine plural)

> **Critical finding: المبتدأ والخبر (subject and predicate) is formally introduced in Grade 4, Unit 2.**

| قراءة أسرية | Play: "فكر قبل أن تفعل" — المشهد الثاني (Scene 2) |

**Full Grammar Scope — Grade 6 (Terms 1 & 2 combined):**
- **النكرة والمعرفة** (Indefinite and definite nouns)
- **أنواع المعارف** (Types of definite nouns)
- **الأسماء الخمسة** (The Five Nouns: أبو، أخو، حمو، فو، ذو) and their special case markers
- **أنواع الخبر** (Types of predicate):
  - الخبر المفرد (Single-word predicate)
  - الخبر الجملة الاسمية (Nominal sentence predicate)
  - الخبر الجملة الفعلية (Verbal sentence predicate)
  - الخبر شبه الجملة (Semi-sentence predicate — prepositional/adverbial)
- **كان وأخواتها** (Kana and its sisters — incomplete verbs) and types of their predicates
- **إن وأخواتها** (Inna and its sisters — emphatic particles) and types of their predicates

> **Critical findings:**
> - **كان وأخواتها is introduced in Grade 6** (Term 2)
> - **إن وأخواتها is introduced in Grade 6** (Term 2, typically after كان)
> - **أنواع الخبر (types of predicate) is taught in Grade 6** as a foundation for understanding how كان and إن modify the predicate
> - **الأسماء الخمسة** with their unique إعراب markers (الواو رفعاً، الألف نصباً، الياء جراً) are a Grade 6 topic

<a id="grammar-summary"></a>
### Grammar (النحو) Scope & Sequence Summary

| Grade | Key Grammar Topics | Notes |
|-------|-------------------|-------|
| **Grade 1** | No formal grammar | Focus on letter recognition and phonics |
| **Grade 2** | No formal grammar | Focus on reading fluency and sentence awareness |
| **Grade 3** | Interrogative particles (أدوات الاستفهام); demonstrative pronouns (أسماء الإشارة); singular/dual/plural recognition; negation particles (لم، لن); prepositions (عن، على); conjunctions (ثم، فـ) | **Introductory awareness only** — not formal إعراب |
| **Grade 4** | **أنواع الكلمة** (word types: اسم، فعل، حرف); **علامات الاسم**; المفرد/المثنى/الجمع; **الضمائر**; **المبتدأ والخبر** (FIRST introduction); المبتدأ والخبر with dual/plural | **Formal grammar begins here**. المبتدأ والخبر introduced in Unit 2. |
| **Grade 5** | **المفعول به** (direct object — all plural types); **الفاعل** (subject of verbal sentence); **المفعول لأجله**; **ظرف الزمان والمكان**; **الأسماء الموصولة**; **علامات الإعراب الفرعية** | Significant deepening of إعراب. Verbal sentences introduced. |
| **Grade 6** | **النكرة والمعرفة**; **الأسماء الخمسة**; **أنواع الخبر** (all 4 types); **كان وأخواتها**; **إن وأخواتها** | Capstone primary grammar. النواسخ (modifiers of الجملة الاسمية) are the culminating topic. |

### Answers to Specific Grammar Questions

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| **When is المبتدأ والخبر introduced?** | **Grade 4, Unit 2** (Term 1). Deepened in Unit 3 with dual/plural forms. Reviewed in Grade 5. |
| **When are إن وأخواتها taught?** | **Grade 6, Term 2.** Taught after أنواع الخبر and after كان وأخواتها. |
| **When are كان وأخواتها taught?** | **Grade 6, Term 2.** Taught after أنواع الخبر (Term 1) as the first set of نواسخ. |
| **When is إعراب introduced?** | Basic awareness in **Grade 3** (recognizing singular/dual/plural). Formal case marking begins in **Grade 4** with المبتدأ والخبر (المرفوعات). Deepens significantly in **Grade 5** with المنصوبات (المفعول به). Full case analysis including subsidiary markers by end of Grade 5. |
| **Verb types (ماضٍ، مضارع، أمر)?** | Introduced as part of **أنواع الكلمة** in **Grade 4** (distinguishing اسم/فعل/حرف). The three verb tenses are formally taught in **Grade 4** and deepened in **Grade 5** (where verbal sentences with الفاعل are introduced). |

### Writing Skills Progression

| Grade | Writing Focus |
|-------|--------------|
| Grade 1 | Letter formation, copying (نسخ), basic word writing |
| Grade 2 | Simple sentences, short paragraphs, basic dictation |
| Grade 3 | Picture story composition (قصة مصورة), alternative story endings, dictation with tanween |
| Grade 4 | Personal letters (رسالة شخصية), concept mapping, structured paragraphs |
| Grade 5 | Formal letters (رسالة رسمية), diary writing (كتابة يوميات), 2 expression topics per term |
| Grade 6 | Informational texts (نصوص معلوماتية), report writing (كتابة تقارير), 2 expression topics per term |

### Spelling/Dictation (الإملاء) Progression

## 3. Christian Religious Education (التربية الدينية المسيحية) — Grades 1-6

### General Structure

- The curriculum is developed by the **Egyptian Ministry of Education** in cooperation with the **Coptic Orthodox Church** and other Egyptian churches.
- All grades follow an **axis-based structure** (محاور): typically **4 axes per year**, 2 per semester.
- Each axis contains **4-6 lessons** (دروس).
- Grades 1-3 use the new system structure aligned with Education 2.0.
- Grades 4-6 follow the subject-based system with units.
- The curriculum was **updated/reformed in 2025-2026** with revised textbooks available from the Ministry's electronic library.
- The curriculum is **not graded/counted in the total score** — it is a pass/fail subject for all primary grades.
- The subject is taught **1 period per week** at all grade levels.

### Common Elements Across All Grades
- **Bible stories** (قصص الكتاب المقدس) from Old and New Testaments
- **Golden verses** (آيات ذهبية) — memorized Scripture passages
- **Memorized passages** — often from the Sermon on the Mount (الموعظة على الجبل)
- **Saints' lives** (سير القديسين) — Coptic Orthodox saints
- **Values and ethics** (القيم والأخلاق)
- **Church traditions** (التقاليد الكنسية)
- **Formative assessments** and **projects** at the end of each unit/axis

### Note on Parallel Curricula
There are **two distinct religious education curricula** in Egyptian schools:
1. **Ministry of Education curriculum** (المنهج الدراسي) — the official textbook used in school, examined in school
2. **Sunday School curriculum** (منهج مدارس الأحد / التربية الكنسية) — used in churches, organized by Coptic Orthodox Church. Sites like St-Takla.org host these church curricula which are separate from the school curriculum.

The information below focuses on the **Ministry of Education school curriculum**.

### Christian RE — Scope & Sequence Summary

| Grade | Bible Focus | Doctrine/Theology | Saints | Values | Church Life |
|-------|------------|-------------------|--------|--------|-------------|
| **1** | Creation, Nativity | God's love, God as Creator | -- | Gratitude, sharing, kindness | Sign of the cross, basic prayers |
| **2** | Creation (deeper), Noah, Abraham, Jesus's miracles | God's care and protection | -- | Honesty, helping, obedience | Baptism awareness, church attendance |
| **3** | Moses, Exodus, Ten Commandments, Parables | God's law and guidance | Intro to Coptic saints | Courage, truthfulness, forgiveness | Liturgy basics, church building |
| **4** | Joshua, Elijah, Deborah, Gideon, Zacchaeus, Virgin Mary/Elisabeth | Prayer, God's will | St. Abanoub, St. George | Love, courage, generosity, self-reliance | Fasting, prayer methods |
| **5** | OT types of Christ (manna, rock, Amalek), Transfiguration, Cana, Three Youths | Christ in OT prophecy, Typology | -- | Courage, patriotism, tech ethics | Tabernacle/church architecture, Sermon on the Mount |
| **6** | Israel's wilderness, Miriam/Aaron/Moses, Onesimus, Saul/Paul | **Holy Trinity**, Bible structure, Repentance & Confession | St. Mercurius, St. Marina | Gratitude, patience, sibling relationships, consultation | Confession as sacrament, Sermon on the Mount |

### Sacraments Coverage

The **Seven Sacraments of the Coptic Orthodox Church** are introduced gradually:
- **Grade 1-2:** Awareness of Baptism (المعمودية)
- **Grade 3-4:** Fasting and Prayer as spiritual disciplines; awareness of the Eucharist through Liturgy
- **Grade 5:** The Tabernacle as precursor to church worship
- **Grade 6:** Repentance and Confession (التوبة والاعتراف) formally taught
- Full sacramental theology continues in preparatory stage (المرحلة الإعدادية)
